Research

Research Statement

ACL tears induce osteoarthritis in over half of those who experience the injury due to changes in the natural knee biomechanics. Osteoarthritis causes pain and limits movement due to the deterioration of cartilage. My research is on whether vibrational therapy on the quadriceps affects the development of osteoarthritis in those who have undergone ACL reconstruction.


Lab Description

The Blackburn Lab is focused on the neuro and biomechanics of knee injuries. In particular, the lab is focusing on researching the factors in development of post ACL reconstruction osteoarthritis.
