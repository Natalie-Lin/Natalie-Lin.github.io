Research

  • Research Statement

    ACL tears induce osteoarthritis in over half of those who experience the injury due to changes in the natural knee biomechanics. Osteoarthritis causes pain and limits movement due to the deterioration of cartilage. My research is on whether vibrational therapy on the quadriceps affects the development of osteoarthritis in those who have undergone ACL reconstruction therapy.

  • Lab Description

    Dr. Troy Blackburn's lab works on rehabilitation strategies for post ACL reconstruction surgery. Several outcomes such as risk of secondary tears and development of osteoarthritis are the focuses of Dr. Blackburn's lab. Right now, Dr. Blackburn is testing a vibrational device that his lab created to stimulate the quadraceps.
